Behind the Brand

PT Hub was created because I wanted more than just workouts — I wanted a system that makes training efficient, structured, and focused. At just 15 years old, I built PT Hub to balance school, running, and the gym while chasing a much bigger dream.

Inspired by HYROX and hybrid athletes, I knew I needed a plan that would get results, keep me disciplined, and prove that age doesn’t limit ambition.

The Vision

PT Hub isn’t just about training plans — it’s about building champions. Every rep, every test, every session is designed to push further. My personal mission is clear: I will be a HYROX World Champion one day.

But PT Hub is for more than me — it’s for anyone who wants to train smarter, get stronger, and become more resilient.

Training Philosophy

  • Efficiency: Every session has a clear purpose.
  • Balance: Strength, conditioning, and running all built in.
  • Progression: Tests every 3 weeks to track performance.
  • Mindset: Discipline always beats motivation.
